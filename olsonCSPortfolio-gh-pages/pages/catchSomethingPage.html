catchSomethingGame ~ Final Project!

My final project involves a variation of the rain catcher game, where a bucket moves around to catch falling raindrops. I have the main elements of this game within my project, such as the moving bucket and falling raindrops. I also added multiple other aspects, such as a timer and reset button, and the ability of the bucket to wrap around the edge of the screen. A lot of my favorite projects have been visual-based, such as the holiday card and chemotaxis game. Therefore, when I saw catchSomethingGame as one of the example projects, it instantly caught my attention because it is an opportunity to use object-oriented programming to create something that is visual and interactive. With this outcome in mind, I chose the catchSomethingGame project idea, and I was inspired to work towards an engaging and entertaining end product.
